Education Experience

[cite: 429]

I earned my Master's in Counseling in Clinical Mental Health Counseling with Drama Therapy from Antioch University in Seattle, WA in Fall 2021. [cite: 429] My master's project included developing a Harry Potter version of D&D called Muggles & Magic for therapy purposes which I have done with various youth populations. [cite: 430] I earned my Bachelor of Science in Psychology and my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theatre from the University of South Dakota in Vermillion, South Dakota in Spring 2018. [cite: 432]

Professional Experience

[cite: 432]

My counseling background involves wraparound intensive services (WISE) counseling, child and family counseling for anyone in the family system, school-based services, group social skill building, and group Dungeons & Dragons therapy. [cite: 433] My experience has equipped me to work with clients experiencing a wide range of issues including depression, suicidal ideation, autism, anxiety, and attention deficit hyperactivity disorder.

services

Individual counseling

I'm a mental health counselor who believes in getting to know you and your interests first, then the healing can begin. [cite: 434] I provide counseling services to teens and adults via telehealth or in person from the Elliot Bay office in Seattle, WA. [cite: 435] I believe that being fun and interest-oriented is going to help find where you are now, so we can build the future you want to achieve - people never fail, plans fail, so let's make a new plan. [cite: 436] You are the expert of your own life; I'm only here to help remember strengths that may have been forgotten or build new supports if helpful. [cite: 437]  I know finding someone to support you on your journey can be hard; I'm honored to support you along your journey. [cite: 438]
